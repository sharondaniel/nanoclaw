# CACC — Correspondence Style Guide

## General Tone
- Formal and professional
- Clear and concise — avoid jargon unless the recipient is technical
- Always acknowledge their message before diving into substance

## Email Structure
1. Greeting — "Dear Ahmed," (not "Hi" or first name only unless established)
2. Brief acknowledgement of their message or context
3. Main content — use numbered lists or bullet points for multi-part responses
4. Clear next steps — who does what by when
5. Closing — "Best Regards,"
   Sharon

## Things to Watch
Ahmed is technical and in the details, he likes to argue and prove he is right. He likes new technologies and new ideas, once I say something, he will keep it and follow up on it.
